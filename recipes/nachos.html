Nachos

Description

Delicous tortilla chips loaded with Beef, lettuce, guac, onions, jalapenos, and melted cheese

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cooked ground beef
  • lettuce
  • veggies
  • melted cheese

Steps

  1. prepare ground beef on the stove
  2. place layer of chips followed by layer of toppings
  3. drizzle cheese
  4. repeat as many layers of chips and toppings as pleased
  5. Enjoy!
