Chicken Alfredo

Description

A delicious italian classic made with cream and parmesan. Cant beat the garlicky taste and creamy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 and 1 half cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 grilled chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Melt butter and cream cheese
  2. pour heavy cream in and bring to a boil
  3. Bring to a boil and pour cheese in
  4. reduce to a simmer and continue to stir
  5. once noodles and chicken are done combine in pot and enjoy!
